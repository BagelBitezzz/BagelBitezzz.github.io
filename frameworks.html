Frameworks

What are Frameworks?

A framework is a tool with the goal of making it easier for a developer to code without having to restart from scratch.

Some popular frameworks include Bootstrap, React, Vue, jQuery, and many more. There is no one correct framework to use, and different projects may be helped by different frameworks.
